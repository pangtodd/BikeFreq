About

Bike Frequency is Foster-Powell's freindly neighborhood bike shop. We've been fixing up bikes for over 10 years, we specialize in repairs and rebuilding classic bikes for everyday use. We love doing this, and people love us- just take a look at our Yelp, Googgle, or Facebook reviews.

Services

All bike services, no job too small. From fixing a flat to rebuilding your bike completely, we can help you!

Sales

We're here for all of your bicycle needs! We can help you get on the road safely within your budget! We offer:

  • classic bikes we've professionally restored
  • locks
  • lights
  • tires and tubes
  • new and used parts
  • tools and bike maintenance items (oil/lubricants, etc)
  • if you need it and we don't have it on hand, we can order it!
