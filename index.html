About

Bike Frequency is Foster-Powell's friendly neighborhood bike shop. With over tweleve years of experience, we specialize in repairing and rebuilding classic bikes for everyday use.

We love doing this, and people love us- just take a look at our Yelp, Google, or Facebook reviews.

Find out for yourself! Visit us next time you have any kind of cycling need, big or small. We're location on the north west corner of SE Foster and SE 59th.

Services

We offer all bike services, no job too small. From fixing a flat to rebuilding your bike completely, we can help you!

sample services/estimates*

  • fix flat tire: $10
  • wheel truing: $25/wheel
  • basic tune up: $75
  • replace chain: $40
  • estimates: free

*these prices represent an estimate/starting point. Actual price may be higher if there are any other underlying problems. We'll let you know.

Sales

We're here for all of your bicycle needs! We can help you get on the road safely within your budget! We offer the following items:

CLASSIC BIKES

Looking for a classic vintage 10 speed? A bomb-proof commuter? Not sure what you need? We have you covered. Check out our selection of used classic bikes we've professionally restored. We'll help you find a bike that fits you and your needs. Including your budget.

LOCKS

Bike thieves suck. We have locks to help keep your ride safe.

LIGHTS

A good rear and front blinker is manditory if you are going to bike at night. Stay safe out there by being seen.

TIRES AND TUBES

Biking in the rain? Make sure you have enough traction to stop safely. We carry the best tires to keep you biking through all seasons.

NEW & USED PARTS

Sometimes used parts work just as well. Sometimes not. We carry both.

TOOLS & MAINTENCE ITEMS

Are you DIYer & need a new bottom-bracket tool? Just need a little oil to keep things running smoothly? Drop on by, we've got it.
