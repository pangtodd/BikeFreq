Bike Frequency. Foster-Powell's neighborhood bike shop.

About

Bike Frequency is Foster-Powell's friendly neighborhood bike shop. With over a dozen years of experience, we specialize in repairing and rebuilding classic bikes for everyday use.

We love doing this, and people love us- just take a look at our Yelp, Google, or Facebook reviews.

Find out for yourself! Visit us next time you have any kind of cycling need, big or small. We're located on the corner of SE Foster and SE 59th. (north side of Foster).

Services

We offer all bike services, no job too small. From fixing a flat to rebuilding your bike completely, we can help you!

sample services/estimates*

  • fix flat tire: starting at $15
  • wheel truing: $25/wheel
  • basic tune up: $75
  • adjust brakes: $5/wheel
  • replace chain: starting at $25
  • estimates: free

*these prices are an estimate/starting point. Actual price may be higher if there are any other underlying problems or if you need specific parts. We'll let you know.

Sales
